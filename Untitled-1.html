Booking
  1. We provide our services within Singapore only.
  2. Please arrange your appointment at least 1 hour in advance.
  3. Once we receive your confirmation, we will arrange the masseuse to your home or hotel within 20-30 minutes.
  4. You can place your booking through our WhatsApp chat, and our support team will verify the booking details with you.
Cancellation Policy
  1. There will be a cancellation fee of $100 or 50% of the package cost, whichever is higher, if you cancel the appointment less than 45 minutes in advance.
Q&A
Q.  Are there any hidden or transportation charges?
There are no hidden charge, and transportation charge is included in the package. all costs are clearly listed.
Q.  Could we possibly extend the massage session by another 30 minutes?
Please feel free to inquire about availability for a subsequent slot, or alternatively, you can schedule another appointment with us and specify your preferences.
Q.  I didn't realize the masseuse had arrived, and she has already left. Can I schedule another appointment?
Certainly, you can check with us the next availability slot. However, Please note that advance payment is required.
We have a 15-minute waiting period. Once our masseuse arrive the provided address and if we do not hear from you, the booking will be cancelled.
