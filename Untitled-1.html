Booking
  1. We provide our services within Singapore only. 
  2.  Please arrange your appointment at least 1 hour in advance.
  3. Once we receive your confirmation, we will arrange masseuse to your home or hotel within 20-30 minutes. 
Cancellation Policy
  1. There will be a cancellation fee of $100 or 50% of the package cost, whichever is higher, if you cancel the appointment less than 45 minutes in advance.
